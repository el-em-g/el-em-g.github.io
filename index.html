My Services

Web Design

Web design is the process of planning, mapping, and arranging content and elements on a website. It's much more than just making things look visually appealing, it's about creating an optimal user experience, ensuring accessibility, and responsiveness on multiple devices.

I enjoy creating interesting and functional websites.

Web Development

Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites and web applications. It involves a combination of coding, programming, and technical skills to create websites that are functional, interactive, and dynamic. While web design focuses on the visual elements, web development focuses on the functional technical side..

A functional site needs good bones.

SEO

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of improving a website's visibility in search engine results to increase organic traffic. The goal of SEO is to help a website rank higher on search engines like Google, Bing, or Yahoo when users search for relevant keywords or phrases. The higher a site ranks, the more likely it is to receive visitors.

Let's make your website visible!

Content Writing

Content writing is the process of creating written material for websites, blogs, social media, and other digital platforms. The goal is to engage the target audience while also aligning with the brand's voice and objectives. Content writers produce a variety of content, including articles, blog posts, and product descriptions, often tailored to specific topics, keywords, or SEO strategies. Good content writing not only delivers valuable information but also encourages user interaction, boosts website traffic, and enhances a brand’s online presence.

Creating engaging content.

Wix Development

Wix development refers to building and customizing websites using Wix, a popular cloud-based website builder platform. Wix allows users to create websites through a drag-and-drop interface, making it simple to learn. However, Wix also provides more advanced tools and features for users who want to dive into custom development and add more complex functionalities.

I can teach you how to manage your own Wix site.
